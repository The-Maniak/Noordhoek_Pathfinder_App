NTM picker - instructions
This is the instruction on NTM picker.

1) Source NTM filename must be in one of the following formats:
- 12wknm23.pdf
- 08wknm22_week08_2022.pdf
Two digits for the week number, four letters and two digits for year name.
This usualy comes as a default, but if for some reason it doesn't please rename the file.
